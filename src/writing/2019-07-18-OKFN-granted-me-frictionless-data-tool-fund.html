--- title: "The OKFN Granted Me a Frictionless Data Tool Fund" ---

I feel honored to announce that the Open Knowledge Foundation (OKFN) has granted me a Frictionless Data Tool fund to improve the landscape of reproducible research.

I will use this one-time $5,000 grant to develop a Google Sheets extension that imports/exports Data Packages.
